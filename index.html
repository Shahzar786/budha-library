Our Story

A Legacy of
Intellectual Excellence

Since 2023

Buddha Library was founded with an unwavering conviction: that access to quality knowledge is the cornerstone of a progressive society. For over two decades, we have served as Patna's most trusted academic institution — providing structured learning environments, curated resources, and mentorship programs that have guided thousands of students toward their aspirations.

Our philosophy transcends the conventional boundaries of a library. We are an ecosystem — where competitive examination preparation, professional skill development, and intellectual curiosity coexist harmoniously.

Academic Integrity
We maintain the highest standards of ethical scholarship and honest academic practice.
Community-Centred

Continuous Innovation

What We Offer

Premium Services

Comprehensive academic infrastructure designed to empower every learner at every stage of their journey.

Study Hall Access

Spacious, air-conditioned reading halls with individual study carrels, high-speed internet, and an environment meticulously optimised for deep focus and concentration.

Competitive Exam Prep

Dedicated preparation programmes for UPSC, BPSC, SSC, Banking, Railways, and other competitive examinations, guided by experienced mentors with proven track records.

Digital Resource Centre

Access to a curated digital library encompassing e-books, academic journals, video lectures, and mock examination portals available round the clock.

Expert Mentorship

Scheduled one-on-one and group mentoring sessions with seasoned educators who provide personalised guidance, study strategies, and performance analytics.

Mock Tests & Analysis

Regularly scheduled mock examinations with detailed performance reports, comparative rankings, and weakness identification to sharpen examination readiness.

Extended Hours Access

Premium members enjoy access from 5:00 AM to midnight, with secure locker facilities, complimentary refreshments, and uninterrupted 24/7 Wi-Fi connectivity.
